Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for international trips, especially during peak seasons. However, we can also accommodate last-minute bookings for many destinations.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer special rates for groups of 6 or more people. Contact us for a customized quote based on your group size and destination.

What is included in your tour packages?

Our packages typically include accommodation, transportation, guided tours, and some meals. Specific inclusions vary by destination and package type.

Can I customize my itinerary?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom itineraries tailored to your interests, budget, and travel style. Our experts will work with you to design the perfect trip.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies by destination and booking type. Generally, we offer flexible cancellation options with different terms for different services.

Do you provide travel insurance?

Yes, we offer comprehensive travel insurance options to protect your investment. We can help you choose the right coverage for your trip.
